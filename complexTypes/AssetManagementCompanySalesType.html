Complex Type: AssetManagementCompanySalesType

Documentation

Company sales data

Child Elements

Name Type Cardinality Documentation
NetSales xs:decimal 1

Net Sales in the Reporting Period (in most cases on a monthly basis) in thousands
of currency
EquityPlaced xs:decimal 1

Equity placed; equivalent to gross sales in open-ended funds. Refers to the month
reported, given in thousands of currency.
