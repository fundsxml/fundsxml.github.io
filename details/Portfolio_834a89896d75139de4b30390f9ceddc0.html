Portfolio

/FundsXML4/RegulatoryReportings/IndirectReporting/TripartiteTemplateSolvencyII/Portfolio

Diagram

Documentation

The Portfolio is analysed from investor's point of view. If Portfolio is a Fund
with share classes, there is one Portfoliofor each share class. For hedged shares, hedging
instruments are included in the prortfolio.
References to Club AMPERE Excel "Template for Solvency 2 reporting": Ann in Annotations refers to row #nn in column A (or "NUM") of he Spreadsheet
