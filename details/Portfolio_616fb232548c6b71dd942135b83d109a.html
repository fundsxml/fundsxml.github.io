Portfolio

/FundsXML4/RegulatoryReportings/IndirectReporting/TripartiteTemplateSolvencyII_V7/Portfolio

Diagram

Documentation

The Portfolio is analysed from investor's point of view. If Portfolio is a Fund
with share classes, there is one Portfolio for each share class. For hedged shares, hedging
instruments are included in the prortfolio.
